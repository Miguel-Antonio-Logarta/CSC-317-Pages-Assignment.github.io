Introduction:

An alien invasion has occured in your city! They are dropping asteroids on you, and the only way to defend yourself is to type out the words. Defend the Earth by launching missiles to destroy the incoming asteroids!

How to Play:

To play the game, you have to type in the input box. When your word matches the word asteroid, press "Enter" to launch a missile and destroy it. Each letter counts as 10 points, however, if you fail to destroy an asteroid, it will lower your healthbar until it reaches zero (Game over).
